Bulls and cows is a game which involves one player guessing a word

For each guess you receive a cow if one of the letters you guessed is in the word

and a bull if you have the correct letter and the correct position in the word

you win the game by getting all of your letters to be bulls and therefore the correct word

you have 5 guesses to guess the letters that make up an unknown word

You will be shown a word which is mixed up and you must guess what word it is based on the letters you can see
